Data Deletion Instructions

According to the Facebook Platform rules, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL. If you want to delete your activities for the Dueli App, you can follow these instructions:

How to Remove Your Data from Dueli

Option 1: Automated Deletion (In-App) 1. Log into your account on Dueli.
2. Go to your Profile Settings.
3. Scroll to the bottom and click the "Delete Account" button.
4. Confirm the action. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data from our servers.
Option 2: Manual Request If you are unable to access your account, you can request data deletion by contacting us directly:

1. Send an email to support@maelsh.com.
2. Subject: Data Deletion Request.
3. Please include your registered email address.
4. We will process your request and delete your data within 7 business days.

What Data is Deleted?

  • Your public profile information (Name, Avatar, Bio).
  • Your login credentials and authentication tokens.
  • Your competition history and comments.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@maelsh.com.
